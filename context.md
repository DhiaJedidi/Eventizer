## 2. What Eventizer is

Eventizer operates at the intersection of **event management** and **digital technology**. It is simultaneously:

- A **full-service event agency** — logistics, hospitality, AV, on-site management from concept to execution
- A **proprietary SaaS platform** — digital registration, QR access control, real-time dashboards, B2B matchmaking, payment tracking
- A **digital communication agency** — social media, PR, branding, A/B-tested creatives
- An **audiovisual production house** — 4K cameras, professional lighting, live editing, AI photobooth

**Core differentiator:** The only operator in Tunisia combining all four pillars under one roof. One point of contact. Zero vendor juggling.

**Proven at scale:** Official digital platform for 1,000+ journalists at the 18th Sommet de la Francophonie, Djerba 2022.

## 3. The 4 pillars

### Pillar 1 — Management Événementiel
- Transfer management: private cars, buses, mobile app seat booking, real-time SMS notifications
- Day-J reception: intelligent participant flow, rapid secure badging, personalized goodies distribution
- Conference room equipment: HD LED screens, pro sound system, scenic lighting, real-time video control room
- Hotel management: sourcing, negotiation, VIP rooming lists, catering coordination, gala dinners
- Exhibition floor plans: 2D optimized layouts, unlimited iterations, full install/deinstall management
- Financial management: real-time budget dashboard, multi-payment tracking (online, wire, cash, cheque)

### Pillar 2 — IT & Tech
- Dev platform: Expert + Senior developers, Agile/Scrum, forfait or régie billing
- Infrastructure & Security: CI/CD pipelines, encryption, backup, VPN, real-time monitoring, compliance audits
- Platform modules: online registration, QR code access control, B2B matchmaking, live dashboards, rooming lists, journalist portal, SMS notifications
- On-site hardware: branded interactive touch kiosks for self-registration

### Pillar 3 — Communication Digitale
- Social media: strategy, content creation, community management, paid campaigns
- Creatives & A/B testing: 3–5 variants minimum, continuous optimization, scale winners
- Public relations & media: journalist relations, press releases, article placement, press conferences, media clipping
- Visual branding: event identity, programs (brochure, booklet, leaflet), signage, totems

### Pillar 4 — Production Audiovisuelle
- Camera fleet: Sony FX3 (4K full-frame hybrid) + Sony A7IV (4K 60fps mirrorless)
- Lighting & sound: decorative and scenic lighting, professional sonorization
- Post-production: same-day on-site video editing, 3-day daily recap videos, full photo/video coverage
- AI-enhanced photobooth: themed AI treatment (Gatsby, 80s, Victorian, Traditional, etc.)
- Pre-event: 3D scenographic simulation of the venue

## 6. Tone of voice

| Principle | What it means in practice |
|---|---|
| **Professional but warm** | Authoritative without being cold. Confident without being arrogant. |
| **Factual over fluffy** | Lead with numbers and outcomes, not adjectives. "250+ événements" not "nombreux événements". |
| **Action-oriented** | Every section ends with something the user can do. No dead ends. |
| **Vous register** | Formal French throughout. Never tutoiement. |
| **Concise** | Max 20 words per sentence. Cut every word that doesn't earn its place. |

## 7. Landing page goals

### Primary goal
Convert a visitor into a **qualified lead** — a quote request, a contact form submission, or a direct WhatsApp/phone contact.

### Secondary goals
- Communicate the full 4-pillar scope without overwhelming
- Establish credibility through concrete case studies and numbers
- Demonstrate the tech edge (platform, Command Hub, real-time data)
- Differentiate from pure agencies (no tech) and pure SaaS tools (no execution)

### Conversion hierarchy
1. **Primary CTA:** "Demandez un devis" → contact form (`#contact`)
2. **Secondary CTA:** "Découvrir nos services" → scroll to pillars (`#services`)
3. **Tertiary CTA:** Direct WhatsApp or phone call
